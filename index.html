Is trying out new food places a hobby? If not, let's make it one.

The Vintage Restaurant at Stone Hill Winery

On a Sunday afternoon, I found myself at the Stone Hill Winery to enjoy some wine and food. The Vintage Restaurant, which once was a carriage house and horse barn, is right by the main building. I ended up getting the German Trio. The dish serves up schweineschnitzel with your choice of sauce, house-marinated beef sauerbraten with a spicy Hermannsberger wine sauce, and a locally produced wurst. Overall the meal was too salty for my liking. I would recommend this to someone who loves really salty food.

Broadway Oyster Bar

I got to enjoy this delicious meal on my birthday this year. I had the Shrimp and Grits with a cup of Chicken and Andouille Gumbo on the side. This place has one of the best gumbos I have tried. Also, I love the atmosphere with the live music.

The Cheesecake Factory
